How it works

From raw idea to shipped feature

01

Connect your Jira ticket - Fred reads everything

Paste a link or open hiFred directly from Jira. Fred reads the ticket, comments, linked designs, and previous decisions — so you never have to repeat yourself or re-explain the context.

02

Fred drafts, you decide - spec done in seconds

Fred turns your context into a structured Live Spec in seconds — acceptance criteria, edge cases, open questions. You stay in control of every decision while Fred handles the heavy lifting.

03

Attach your Figma screens - Fred reasons visually

Attach your Figma screens and Fred reasons visually — mapping UI states, flows, and edge cases directly into the spec. No more translating designs into requirements by hand.

04

Catch gaps before engineers do

Fred flags ambiguities, missing states, and conflicting requirements before a single line of code is written. Ship fewer surprises.

05

Engineers ask questions. Fred answers. You sleep.

Engineers ask questions directly in the ticket. Fred answers instantly from the spec — so engineers never wait, and you're never interrupted.

06

The full spec, inside Cursor. No context switching.

Fred's Live Specs are available directly inside Cursor. Engineers get the full context — requirements, edge cases, decisions — without ever leaving their editor.
